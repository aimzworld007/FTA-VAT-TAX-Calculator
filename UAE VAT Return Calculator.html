5. VAT Guidance Notes

Screen Only
  • VAT Inclusive: Total already includes VAT. Example AED 105 = AED 100 value + AED 5 VAT. Formula: VAT = Total × 5 / 105.
  • VAT Exclusive: VAT is not included yet. Example AED 100 + 5% VAT = AED 105 total.
  • Recoverable VAT: Claim only business expenses with valid UAE tax invoices and supplier TRN.
  • Non-claimable expenses: Do not claim VAT on personal, entertainment, or non-business expenses.
  • Supermarket note: If you have zero-rated/exempt items, separate them before filing.

6. Filing Checklist

Screen Only
  • Sales report reconciled with POS and bank/cash collections.
  • Purchase and expense invoices checked with TRN and tax invoice format.
  • Zero-rated, exempt, and standard-rated sales separated correctly.
  • Adjustments documented before final submission.
  • Final values reviewed by accountant/auditor before filing.
