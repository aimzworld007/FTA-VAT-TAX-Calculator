VAT Guidance Notes

Keep these notes with your report.

VAT Inclusive

VAT is already included inside the total amount. Example: AED 105 includes AED 100 taxable value + AED 5 VAT. Formula: VAT = total × 5 ÷ 105.

VAT Exclusive

VAT is not included yet. Example: AED 100 taxable value + AED 5 VAT = AED 105 total. Formula: VAT = amount × 5%.

Emirate Boxes

Standard rated sales are reported by emirate: Abu Dhabi 1a, Dubai 1b, Sharjah 1c, Ajman 1d, Umm Al Quwain 1e, Ras Al Khaimah 1f, Fujairah 1g.

Recoverable VAT

Claim input VAT only when you have valid UAE tax invoices, the expense is business related, and the invoice belongs to the VAT period.
